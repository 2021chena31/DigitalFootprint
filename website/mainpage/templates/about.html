About this Project

Privacy online is important. This app aims to make privacy accessible to everyone, regardless of familiarity with the internet.


This webapp works by scraping the web for information related to what the user inputs. You do not need to fill all the fields.

However, the more fields filled, the more likely you will find information relevant to you. No information is stored about the users.


Ways to keep your privacy online

1. Limit the information you share about yourself online. Things such as birthdates

2. Use incognito mode. Incognito mode (along with keeping your browing history clear), won't save your cookies.
